#1 NICE TO MEET YOU
We are a family based in Kirkland, Washington, exploring the idea of a small, home-inspired child care setting in the Moss Bay area in 2026. As parents of four, we believe children flourish in calm, familiar, home-like environments. YOLM reflects this belief — warm, personal, and intentionally simple.
#2 YOLM CURRICULUM
YOLM is a values-led concept centered on care, emotional safety, and human connection. Our inspiration comes from Nordic family life, where love and trust are woven into everyday routines. At this stage, YOLM represents a philosophy rather than a formal program.
#3 OUR STORY
Jenni and Kristian are originally from Estonia and are now raising their four children in the Pacific Northwest. Estonian language, music, and cultural education are an intentional and meaningful part of our family life, actively practiced with our children. This cultural foundation naturally shapes the long-term vision and identity behind YOLM.
#4 COOPERATION
YOLM is currently in an early exploration phase. We welcome conversations with individuals and families who care about Estonian language, culture, and community on the U.S. West Coast. If this vision resonates with you, we would be glad to connect and exchange ideas as the concept evolves.
